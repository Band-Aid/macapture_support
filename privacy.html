Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

macapture ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information macapture collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

macapture does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data or captured content. All screenshots and recordings are saved directly to your device and are never sent to our servers.

When you contact us through our support page, we receive only the information you voluntarily provide (such as your name, email address, and message). This information is used solely to respond to your support request and is not shared with third parties.

Permissions

macapture requests the following macOS permissions:

  • Screen Recording — required to capture screenshots and screen recordings. Captured content stays on your device.
  • Microphone (optional) — used only when you explicitly choose to record audio. Audio is included only in the recording you save locally.

Analytics

macapture does not use third-party analytics services or tracking libraries. We do not collect crash reports, usage statistics, or any telemetry data.

Third-Party Services

macapture is distributed through the Mac App Store, which is operated by Apple Inc. Apple's privacy practices are governed by the Apple Privacy Policy. We do not have access to any purchase or account information held by Apple.

Children's Privacy

macapture does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support form.
