Privacy Policy

Macapture

Macapture does not collect, sell, rent, transmit, or share your personal data. The app is designed to work locally on your Mac.

What Macapture does not collect

  • No account information
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No analytics or advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No usage tracking sent to any server
  • No screenshots, recordings, clipboard items, or annotations uploaded off your device

How the app works

Macapture captures screenshots and screen recordings locally on your Mac. If you enable optional features such as clipboard history, that information is processed and stored locally on your device only. Macapture does not operate a cloud service and does not transmit user content to the developer or to third parties.

Permissions

Macapture requests only the macOS permissions needed for features you use, such as Screen Recording access for screenshots and video capture. These permissions are managed by macOS and can be changed at any time in System Settings.

Local-only design. Screenshots, videos, history items, and settings stay on your Mac unless you explicitly move, export, or share them yourself.

Third parties

Macapture does not include third-party advertising, third-party analytics, or external data collection SDKs.

Changes

If the privacy behavior of Macapture changes in the future, this policy should be updated to reflect those changes before release.
