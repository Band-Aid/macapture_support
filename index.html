Macapture

Lightweight macOS screen capture with video recording, image annotation, and clipboard history.

Requirements

  • macOS 14.0 or later
  • Screen Recording permission (prompted on first use)

Features

  • Screenshot Capture — area selection with multi-monitor support, saved as PNG and auto-copied to clipboard
  • Video Recording — configurable frame rate (5–60 fps), export as MP4 (H.264) or GIF, animated click-ripple overlay
  • Image Editor — annotate captures with freehand draw, arrows, rectangles, text labels, and pixelate redaction
  • History Panel — slide-out panel with search, thumbnails, quick actions (open, reveal in Finder, copy), up to 200 items
  • Clipboard History — optional text clipboard monitoring that appears in the history panel
  • Menu Bar — status icon for quick access to the main window, settings, and quit

Global Keyboard Shortcuts

Default ShortcutAction
⇧⌘6Open main window
⇧⌘CCapture area
⇧⌘VCapture video
⇧⌘HToggle history

All shortcuts are configurable in Settings. Conflicts are auto-resolved.
